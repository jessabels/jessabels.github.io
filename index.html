About Me

My name is Jessica Abels. I am currently a student at App Academy, where I am studying Full-Stack Software Engineering. I've always been fascinated with all things computer related, since I was a little kid. I studied web development at Portland Community College and became familiar with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript basics. After finishing up my Associates Degree, I got my first job in the tech industry as a Technical Support Agent. After that, I got my first job in Quality Assurance where I learned how to do some UI Automation. Working alongside other developers, I was always amazed by their abilities and soon decided that I wanted to be just like them. Here I am now, learning how to build cool things!
